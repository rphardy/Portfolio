A Reproducible Exploratory Data Analysis using R

This Rpubs publication details an analysis that can be fully reproduced, in order to arrive at a data-driven recommendation.

Results reveal the areas for the US government to target in developing strategies to mitigate damage to property and population health arising from extreme weather events across the US.
